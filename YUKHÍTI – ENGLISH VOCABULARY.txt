 YUKHÍTI – ENGLISH VOCABULARY                            
                                                                                    
               The vocabulary contains all the words found in Gatschet and Swanton (1932), as well as
                                                                                    
          those few words missed by Swanton, but to be found in the original field notes of Albert S.
                                                                                    
          Gatschet. The words are given in their phonemically reconstructed forms, including vowel
                                                                                    
          length and accentuation. In the alphabetical order, long vowels precede short vowels, the
                                                                                    
          phoneme n̥ is placed after n, and the phoneme ƛ and its variant ł are placed after t. The sounds s
                                                                                    
          and š are alphabetized together.                                          
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
               A compound word is listed under the basic elements that make up the compound. In
                                                                                    
          cases where an element does not occur as an independent word, it is listed without a definition.
                                                                                    
          A few words are reconstructed whenever they can clearly be derived from an attested word.
                                                                                    
          These reconstructed words are marked with an asterisk.                    
                                                                                    
               The following are the abbreviations used:                            
                                                                                    
          adj       adjective                                                       
                                                                                    
          adv       adverb                                                          
                                                                                    
          deic      deictic                                                         
                                                                                    
          intj      interjection                                                    
                                                                                    
                                                                                    
          iv        intransitive verb                                               
                                                                                    
          n         noun                                                            
                                                                                    
          pp        postposition                                                    
                                                                                    
          pro       pronoun                                                         
                                                                                    
          sv        stative verb                                                    
                                                                                    
          tv        transitive verb                                                 
                                                                                    
          var       variant form
